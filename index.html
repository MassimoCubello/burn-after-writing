Burn After Writing

Stewing on that email you want to write? Scared to hit send? Burn it instead. Write your email here, and when you're done, click "Burn" to see it disappear forever.

This is a safe space to vent, rant, or just get your thoughts out without the fear of judgment or consequences. Your words will be gone forever, so let it all out!

Oh, and you may even get a response.
